The columns are:
- name of the legal act, composed of the abbreviation of the country followed by the abbreviation of the legal act
- creation method of the graph (tokens = set of words; stems = set of stemmed words; token pairs = set of pairs of words; stem pairs = set of pairs of stemmed words)
- number of articles / paragraphs of the legal act
- density of the graph
-  minimal degree of the nodes
- maximal degree of the nodes
- mean degree of the nodes
- median degree of the nodes
-  minimal distance of the edges
- maximal distance of the edges
- mean distance of the edges
- median distance of the edges
-  minimal strength of the edges
- maximal strength of the edges
- mean strength of the edges
- median strength of the edges
